People

The team behind the research

AETHER brings together researchers with a broad range of engineering expertise, united by a shared focus on healthcare.

Leadership

Academic staff

Dr Mohammad Eissa

Academic Lead, AETHER Group

Dr Eissa leads the AETHER Group at the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, University of Sheffield. His research applies machine learning and data-driven methods to healthcare, including blood glucose analysis and behaviour change in type 1 diabetes, spanning the group's themes of artificial intelligence, medical devices, and automatic control.

Prof Mohammed Benaissa

Professor of Information Engineering

Prof Benaissa's research spans hardware and embedded systems and machine learning for healthcare. Within the group's work this includes non-invasive glucose sensing from infrared spectroscopy, data-driven blood glucose prediction, and digital biomarkers for conditions such as Alzheimer's disease and depression.
