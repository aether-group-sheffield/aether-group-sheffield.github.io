Good to know

Before you write

A short message is all we need: who you are, what you're working on, and what kind of input you're looking for. If your enquiry relates to a specific research theme, such as artificial intelligence, medical devices, automatic control, or hardware, mentioning it helps us route your message to the right group member faster.

For prospective PhD students: include a brief CV and a few lines on the research areas that interest you. You don't need a fully formed proposal to start the conversation.
